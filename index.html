  • It will get session taken from the request header and gets login user details. And assign that user as owner of the developer company.

/developercompanies/{developercompanyid}/approvaldecision

It approves or rejects a developer company. If the developer company is approved the status of the developer company is changed to ACTIVE and if the developer company is rejected the status of the developer company is changed to REJECTED. An email is sent to the owner of the developer company with the approval decision. A developer company can be approved or rejected only if its current status is SUBMITTED.

/developercompanies/{developercompanyid}/accounts/{username}

Dissociates an account from a specific developer company. Only the accounts associated with a developer company in "ADMIN" role can perform this operation for that developer company. The {developerCompanyId} is a value that uniquely identifies a developer company. The {username} is a value that uniquely identifies an account. The {developerCompanyId} and {username} together uniquely identify a developer-company-account association.

/developercompanies/{developercompanyid}/accounts/{username} delete

delete

Dissociates an account from a specific developer company. Only the accounts associated with a developer company in "ADMIN" role can perform this operation for that developer company. The {developerCompanyId} is a value that uniquely identifies a developer company. The {username} is a value that uniquely identifies an account. The {developerCompanyId} and {username} together uniquely identify a developer-company-account association.

NOTE: An owner account can not be dissociated from the developer company.
